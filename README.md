## About the Project

This project analyzes customer data from AeroFit, a leading brand in fitness equipment, to develop customer profiles and provide insights into treadmill buyer characteristics. The analysis helps identify target audiences for different treadmill products and informs marketing and product placement strategies.

## Data Description

The company collected data on individuals who purchased a treadmill from the AeroFit stores during the prior three months. The dataset `aerofit_treadmill_data.csv` has the following features:

- **Product** - product purchased: KP281, KP481, or KP781
- **Age** - in years
- **Gender** - male/female
- **Education** - in years
- **MaritalStatus** - single or partnered
- **Usage** - the average number of times the customer plans to use the treadmill each week
- **Fitness** - self-rated fitness on a 1-5 scale, where 1 is poor shape and 5 is excellent shape
- **Income** - annual income in US dollars
- **Miles** - the average number of miles the customer expects to walk/run each week
